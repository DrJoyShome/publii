Groundbreaking Chronic Total Occlusion Conclave

BM Birla Heart Hospital recently hosted the Chronic Total Occlusion (CTO) Conclave, bringing together global experts to discuss innovative treatment techniques.

Doctor Kenji Kawajiri, Chief of the Heart Centre at Marsubara Tokuausukai Hospital, Japan, shared valuable insights on advanced CTO management.

Doctor Joy Shome presented a challenging CTO case, sparking meaningful discussions.

The conclave was a key step in the endeavour to advance CTO care, emphasise collaboration and cutting-edge techniques for better patient outcomes.
